About Us

YouthMind Uganda was founded to address rising mental health challenges among young people. We create safe spaces, give free resources and do outreach in schools and communities.

Our Goals

  • Increase mental health awareness
  • Provide free counseling resources
  • Support the youth through workshops and campaigns
  • Fight stigma around mental health
